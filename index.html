About Messi

Lionel Messi was born, 24 June 1987, in Rosario, Argentina to a working-class family. His father was a factory steel worker, and his mother a cleaner. He was encouraged by his grandmother to play in his brothers soccer league when they were down a player and just like that Messi was Born.
